How It Works

Four steps to faster grading.

From video upload to finalized evaluation in minutes.

1

Students Upload

Students record and upload speech videos directly. Stored securely in FERPA-compliant cloud storage.

2

AI Analyzes

Vision-language model watches and evaluates against your custom rubric, assessing delivery, content, and organization.

3

You Review

Watch video side-by-side with the rubric. Adjust scores and refine feedback as needed.

4

System Learns

Your corrections automatically train the AI. Over time, accuracy improves and adjustments decrease.
