Design Recommendation:

For desktop/web app icons, look for the logo that remains recognizable at 16×16 (favicon size) and has good contrast at small sizes. The 3D logo typically performs best because depth and shadows help maintain recognition when scaled down. However, the best choice depends on which logo maintains its core shape and readability at the smallest size.
